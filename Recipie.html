Kadai Panner

To prepare this flavorful dish, you'll need 250 grams of paneer, 2 large tomatoes, 1 large onion, 1 green bell pepper (capsicum), 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 2-3 green chilies, 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), 1 tablespoon oil, and salt to taste. Start by heating oil in a kadai (a type of Indian wok) and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Next, add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cooking until the raw smell disappears. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil separates. Mix in the coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes before adding diced bell pepper and paneer cubes. Stir well and let the mixture cook for about 5-7 minutes until the paneer absorbs the flavors. Sprinkle garam masala and kasuri methi, give it a final mix, and cook for another minute. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Chicken Lollipos

To prepare chicken lollipops, you'll need 12 chicken wings, 1/2 cup of flour, 2 beaten eggs, 1 cup of breadcrumbs, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of chili sauce, 1 tablespoon of garlic paste, 1 tablespoon of ginger paste, salt, and oil for frying. Begin by trimming the chicken wings and pushing the meat towards one end to create a lollipop shape. Marinate the wings with soy sauce, chili sauce, garlic paste, ginger paste, and a pinch of salt for at least 30 minutes. Dredge the marinated wings in flour, dip them in the beaten eggs, and coat them with breadcrumbs. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the wings until they are golden brown and crispy. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Chicken Noodles

To make a comforting bowl of chicken noodles, start by gathering the ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, 8 ounces of egg noodles, 2 cups of chicken broth, 1 cup of diced carrots, 1 cup of diced celery, 1 diced onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon of dried thyme, 1 teaspoon of dried parsley, salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Begin by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, cooking until they are softened, about 5 minutes. Pour in the chicken broth, then add the thyme and parsley, bringing the mixture to a boil. While waiting for the broth to boil, shred the cooked chicken. Once boiling, add the egg noodles and cook according to the package instructions. When the noodles are nearly done, return the shredded chicken to the pot, season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chicken noodles.

Choclate Milk shake

To make a delicious chocolate milkshake, you'll need the following ingredients: 2 cups of vanilla ice cream, 1 cup of milk, 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup, and whipped cream for topping (optional). Begin by combining the ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup in a blender. Blend the mixture until it's smooth and creamy. If you prefer a thicker milkshake, you can add more ice cream, or if you want it thinner, add a bit more milk. Once blended to your desired consistency, pour the milkshake into glasses. Top with whipped cream and an extra drizzle of chocolate syrup if you like. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing chocolate treat!

Pizza

To make a delicious sweetcorn pizza, start by preparing your pizza dough or using a store-bought crust. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the crust and sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Add a cup of cooked sweetcorn kernels, distributing them evenly across the pizza. Optionally, add thinly sliced red onions, bell peppers, or jalapeños for extra flavor and texture. Season with a pinch of salt, pepper, and a dash of oregano. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Once out of the oven, let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your sweetcorn pizza hot with a sprinkle of fresh basil or a drizzle of olive oil.
